Overview

Many writers who write for hobby have trouble writing as frequently and consistently as they wish.
They must also maintain a balance between writing and editing.

For new writers, improving their writing quality often means tackling these two issues.
Without any external pressure or support, these writers who are usually in charge of their own goals and deadlines often also succumb to lethargy and procrastination.

TagLine is the solution that gathers what writers need in a single word processor.
Ultimately, TagLine keeps writers writing.
