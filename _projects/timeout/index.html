TimeOut

Stressed? Bored at work?

Nowadays, we are spending less and less time outdoors. Many people are working a desk job that requires them to stay indoors during their work hours. Only a fraction have the luxury of spending time outside during work and actually enjoying it.

Solution

Our solution is a mobile application that motivates people to go outside by allowing them to schedule outside activities with their friends and set personal goals. Furthermore, our users can benefit from proposed goals based on research. These specific goals can help the user to improve his/her mental and physical health.
