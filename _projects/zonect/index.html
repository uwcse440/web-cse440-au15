Zonect

A mobile video messaging application to help connect loved ones across time zones.

The Problem Space

Despite our best efforts to stay connected to the people we care about, we frequently lose touch with them when they move away. When you can’t be physically present in someone’s life, you grow apart to the point where it becomes too awkward and/or uncomfortable to start a conversation. We've designed a mobile video messaging application to help people maintain these long-distance relationships.

What Can Zonect Do?

Schedule when you want to send a video

Zonect picks the best time to send your loved one a video you've recorded based on what time zone they're in.

Interactively Comment on Videos

Our app allows you to add emojis, comments, and audio recordings directly on your your videos.

Discover Common Interests

Get suggestions for videos to watch to learn more about your contacts.
